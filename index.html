Phani Teja Singamaneni

Phd Candidate at LAAS-CNRS

Bonjour !! I am currently a PhD candidate at LAAS-CNRS under Dr. Rachid Alami, working on Human Robot Interaction, specifically, exploring learning in the field of Human Aware Navigation or Motion Planning. My research interests include Motion Planning and Control, Reinforcement Learning, Imitation Learning and Humanoid robotics. Previously, I worked at IIIT-Hyderabad as a Masters researcher under the guidance of Dr. Madhava Krishna and Dr. Abhishek Sarkar. My Masters Dissertation topic was “Learning Multi-Goal Reachability in a Humanoid Robot using Deep Reinforcement Learning”.
